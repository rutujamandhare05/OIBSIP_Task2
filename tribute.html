Stephan Hawking

Master of Universe

Stephan Hawking was an English Theoretical physicist, cosmologist and author. At the time of his death, he was director of research at the center of Theoretical Cosmology at the University of Cambridge.

Here's a research done by Stephan Hawking:

  • Major Contributions to the field of general relativity
  • Proved the basic theorems on the lwas governing black holes
  • The interpretation of cosmological observations and to the design of gravitational wave detectors
  • Gravitational singularity theorems ih the framework of general relativity
  • The theoretical prediction thet black holes emit radiation, often called Hawking radiation
  • Set out a theory of cosmology explained by a union of general theory of relativity and quantum mechanics
  • His book A Brief History of Time appeared on the Sunday Times bestseller list for record breaking 237 weeks
